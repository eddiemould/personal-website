My Work

I am a London-based, aspiring film and television editor, currently cutting short films and getting started in the industry as a trainee.


I have been trained in Avid Media Composer and am proficient in DaVinci Resolve and the Adobe Creative Suite.


I have cut both animation and live action - I am particularly interested in animation! Other languages I have edited in include British Sign Language (BSL) and Spanish.


I studied at Bournemouth Film School where I cut and worked on a number of short films, advertisements and music videos.


I am a student member of the British Film Editors and one of the Minutes Secretaries for The Rough Assembly (the Post Production & Facilities Branch of Bectu).


You can reach me via email: post@eddiemould.com
