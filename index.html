Deaf, Profoundly (2024)

Dir. Calvin Lupton

An intimate exploration of a fractured and strained relationship between a young adult and his profoundly deaf mother. Following a crucial few days in Oscar’s life the audience are invited to hold his hand as he begins to learn how to talk about his feelings, by unearthing the deaf experience.
